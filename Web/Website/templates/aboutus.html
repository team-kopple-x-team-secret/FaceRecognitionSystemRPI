About the System

The goal of this system is to create an attendance system based on face recognition methods. In this method, a person's face will be taken into account while recording attendance. Due to its growing use and popularity, face recognition will have a significant influence on the university.

Objectives

The main objective of the websites is to identify the faculty faces accurately, to mark the attendance automatically, to reduce the time and the efforts required for manual attendance to provide a valuable attentive system for both teacher and students, and lastly is to provides flexibility and reduces time loss.
